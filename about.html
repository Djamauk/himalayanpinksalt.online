Our Story

Salt of the Earth was founded with a passion for bringing the world's finest Himalayan pink salt to your table. From the pristine mountains of the Himalayas to your kitchen, we are committed to delivering a product that is rich in minerals and natural flavor.

Our team travels directly to the source to ensure ethical sourcing and the highest quality standards. Join us on our journey as we continue to explore the wonders of this ancient crystal.
