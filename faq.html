Frequently Asked Questions

Where is your salt sourced?

Our Himalayan pink salt is mined from ancient salt deposits in the foothills of the Himalayan mountains.

Is your salt organic?

Himalayan salt is a natural mineral and does not need to be certified organic. It is free from additives and processed only through washing and drying.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on location.
